About Content Sync

Content Sync allows you to connect your accounts from various platforms and automatically sync your data to your profile.

Benefits:

  • Centralized profile with all your activity
  • Track your gaming and entertainment habits
  • Sync data automatically
  • Resync anytime with one click

How It Works

  1. Click "Connect" for any service
  2. Follow the authentication steps
  3. Your data will sync automatically
  4. Click "Resync Now" to update anytime
